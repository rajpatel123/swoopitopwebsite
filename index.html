About

Who we are

Here are some benefits of using the Swoop It Up app:

  • We provide jobs for a wide range of individuals: those considered to be the working class, students and individuals who are looking for extra income to support themselves and/or their families.
  • Swoop It Up is here to make it easier for consumers to return their items. Whether it needs to be dropped off at the post office, or you need an item delivered to someone that day. Let us help you!
  • We are here to help both businesses and consumers, by taking the responsibility of transporting their items to the right destination.

Swoop It Up is the future of transportations for your everyday items. Have you been meaning to return packages, and have gone for weeks or even months without returning them? The Swoop It Up App will take care of this problem for you! This service is run by a app that you can download for free on any handheld device. A Swooper (independent contractors) will pick up your items, and deliver them to your desired destination; whether you are a consumer or run a business, Swoop It Up can meet your needs!

INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Through Swoop It Up, you will learn how to become a top independent contractor, learn tax benefits, as well as learn the ropes of being an entrepreneur.

STANDARD SWOOP IT UP

Standard Swoop It Up order is your basic order. A Swooper will pick up your package, and drop it off to your requested destination; however, they will also have the option to pick up multiple packages on the way to your drop off destination.

EXPRESS SWOOP IT UP

Express Swoop It Up order; you can schedule a pick up for your item. After the contracted Swooper picks up your item, they will go directly to your designated drop-off destination.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I sign up as a Swooper?

    Download the Swooper driver app on your smart phone. We are available on both IOS or Android, and it is an easy click register.

  • Can I see where my driver is going?

    Yes, you will have a visual navigational map on where your item is being delivered.

  • What is the age limit to be a Swooper?

    14 years of age and up. If you are under 18, you will need a parent or guardian’s signature when signing up.

  • How can I order a Swoop It Up?

    Download the Swoop It Up app on a smart phone (IOS or Android) and book a pick Up.

  • What type of vehicle do I need?

    Any type of mobile vehicle that can carry a package up to 50lbs and shorter than 50 Pounds. For example: a bike, a scooter, a car, a SUV, or truck.
